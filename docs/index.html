About the Project

The Bus Booking System is a comprehensive platform designed to simplify the process of booking bus tickets online. Users can search for available routes, select buses, choose seats, and complete their booking with ease. The system ensures a seamless and user-friendly experience, making bus travel more convenient than ever.

This project was inspired by the need to provide a more efficient and accessible way for people to book bus tickets. During my University days, I used bus services to book and travel through a company based near my area. The company relied on a manual booking process where in order for users to book, they had to physically visit their offices, where the person in charge would check for manual recordings if there are any buses available. If any buses available, one would be Issued a physical ticket written on a piece of paper to show during travelling day. The customer then proceeds to paying using physical cash.

While this method is effective and serves it's purpose, it is cumbersome both to the User and the administrators. Users have to be physically present during the whole booking process and errors are prone during the process. This arises the need to streamline that process, where users can book tickets from the comfort of their homes.

This project is also a part of my portfolio for Holberton School, demonstrating my skills in full-stack development.
